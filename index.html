Why Martial Arts Should be a Part of Everyone's Life!

Martial-to, or suited for war or a warrior, relating to an army or to military life, experienced in or inclined to war, warlike

Arts-something that is created with imagination and skill and that is beautiful or that expresses important ideas or feelings

Martial Arts are codified systems and traditions of combat practices, which are practiced for a variety of reasons: self-defense, competition, physical health and fitness, entertainment, as well as mental, physical, and spiritual development"

What these definitions do not spell out are the benefits of practicing and studying the martial arts, many of us think of Bruce Lee , Karate flicks or UFC/MMA. Simply its associated with some type of entertainment or violent act. This could not be further from the truth. Martial arts is a great way to create a disciplined lifestyle as well as a spiritual one.

Here is a list of The benefits of Martial Arts :

  • Physical Fitness
  • Self Esteem, Confidence, and Character Building
  • Sense of Community
  • Increased Discipline and Life Management
  • Self Defense
  • Benefits to Education in Children
